Your Right to Delete Your Data

Restaurant SaaS respects your privacy. You have the right to request deletion of any personal data we hold about you. This page explains what data we store and how to request its deletion.

What Data We May Hold

  • Restaurant owners: Account email, hashed password, restaurant name, menu data, subscription info.
  • End-customers (via messaging channels): Name, phone number, delivery address (only if provided during an order), and conversation history with the restaurant's AI chatbot.
  • Platform interaction logs: API access logs retained for security purposes (typically 30 days).

We do not store Facebook/Instagram personal profile data beyond a sender ID used for message routing.

How to Request Deletion

Send an email to our support address with the subject line "Data Deletion Request":

Email: cdasplay@gmail.com

Please include:

  • Your account email or phone number (for end-customer requests)
  • The name of the restaurant whose chatbot you interacted with (if applicable)
  • Any other identifying information to help us locate your data
Send Deletion Request by Email

What Happens After Your Request

  • We will acknowledge your request within 3 business days.
  • Deletion will be completed within 30 days of the request.
  • We will send a confirmation email once deletion is complete.
  • Some data may be retained if legally required (e.g., billing records required by tax law).

Facebook / Instagram Data Deletion

If you connected to a restaurant's chatbot via Facebook or Instagram and want your data removed from our platform, you can also initiate deletion directly through Facebook's settings:

  • Go to Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites
  • Find Restaurant SaaS and click Remove
  • This will trigger an automatic deletion callback to our system

Alternatively, send us an email as described above and we will process your request manually.
