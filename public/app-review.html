2. Platform Purpose

Restaurant SaaS is an AI-powered customer support and order-taking platform for restaurants. It connects restaurants to their customers via WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook Messenger, and Telegram, providing:

  • An AI chatbot (powered by OpenAI GPT-4o-mini) that handles customer inquiries, answers menu questions, and collects orders in natural language.
  • A web dashboard for restaurant owners to manage products, view orders, monitor conversations, and configure the chatbot.
  • Multi-channel channel management with per-channel enable/disable and webhook management.
  • Analytics and conversation history for restaurant staff.
  • Subscription management and billing workflow.

The platform is used only by verified restaurant business owners who subscribe to the service. End-users are the restaurant's own customers who message the restaurant via its existing social media or WhatsApp accounts.

3. Meta Platform API Usage

WhatsApp Business Platform

Used to receive and send messages on behalf of restaurant owners' WhatsApp Business numbers.

PermissionTypeReason
whatsapp_business_messaging Required Send and receive customer messages on behalf of the restaurant's WhatsApp Business number.
whatsapp_business_management Required Register webhook, manage phone number settings, and read WABA configuration.

Instagram Messaging

Used to receive and send direct messages on behalf of restaurant owners' Instagram Business accounts.

PermissionTypeReason
instagram_basic Required Identify the connected Instagram Business account.
instagram_manage_messages Required Read incoming customer DMs and send automated replies from the AI chatbot.
pages_show_list Required List Pages linked to the user's Instagram account during OAuth setup.

Facebook Messenger

Used to receive and send Messenger messages on behalf of restaurant owners' Facebook Pages.

PermissionTypeReason
pages_messaging Required Send and receive Messenger messages on behalf of the connected Facebook Page.
pages_manage_metadata Required Subscribe to webhooks for incoming Messenger events.
pages_read_engagement Optional Read Page engagement data for analytics display in the dashboard.

4. User Data Handling

  • We access only the messages that customers actively send to the restaurant's connected channel.
  • We do not access personal timelines, friend lists, or any data beyond messaging.
  • Conversation data is visible only to the restaurant owner who owns the channel.
  • Data is not shared with other tenants (restaurants) on the platform.
  • We do not use conversation data for advertising or profiling.
  • Data deletion requests are handled at: https://restaurant-saas-1.onrender.com/data-deletion

5. Technical Implementation

  • Backend: Python / FastAPI, deployed on Render (render.yaml in repository)
  • Database: PostgreSQL (production) / SQLite (development)
  • AI: OpenAI GPT-4o-mini for chatbot response generation
  • Webhook base URL: https://restaurant-saas-1.onrender.com
  • OAuth callback URL: https://restaurant-saas-1.onrender.com/api/integrations/oauth/callback
  • WhatsApp webhook: /api/webhooks/whatsapp/{restaurant_id}
  • Instagram webhook: /api/webhooks/instagram/{restaurant_id}
  • Facebook webhook: /api/webhooks/facebook/{restaurant_id}
