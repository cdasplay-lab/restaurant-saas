Privacy Policy

Restaurant SaaS — AI-powered customer support for restaurants

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

Restaurant SaaS ("we", "us", or "our") operates a multi-tenant SaaS platform that helps restaurant businesses automate customer support and order-taking through messaging channels including WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook Messenger, and Telegram.

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data. By using our platform, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: Name, email address, phone number, and password (hashed) when a restaurant registers on our platform.
  • Restaurant data: Menu items, product details, pricing, and business information entered by restaurant owners.
  • Customer messages: Messages sent by end-customers through connected channels (WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, Telegram) for the purpose of automated response and order processing.
  • Order data: Orders extracted from customer conversations, including items ordered and order status.
  • Channel credentials: Access tokens and configuration data necessary to connect messaging platforms. These are stored securely and never exposed publicly.
  • Usage data: Log data, API request metadata, and analytics used to improve the service.

3. How We Use Data

We use collected data solely to operate and improve the platform:

  • To power the AI chatbot and respond to customer messages on behalf of restaurants.
  • To process and track orders placed through messaging channels.
  • To deliver analytics and performance reports to restaurant owners.
  • To authenticate users and secure access to dashboard features.
  • To maintain service reliability and debug technical issues.

We do not use customer messages for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond providing the service the restaurant has configured.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data. Data is shared only in these cases:

  • OpenAI: Customer messages are sent to OpenAI's API to generate automated replies. OpenAI's data usage policies apply. Messages are not used to train OpenAI models under the API terms.
  • Meta Platforms (Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp): We exchange data with Meta APIs as required to send and receive messages through connected channels.
  • Telegram: Messages are exchanged via the Telegram Bot API in accordance with Telegram's terms of service.
  • Cloud infrastructure: Data is hosted on secure cloud providers (Render, Supabase) under standard data processing agreements.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

5. Data Retention

We retain data for as long as a restaurant account is active. Conversation messages and order history are kept to allow restaurant owners to review past interactions. If a restaurant account is deleted, associated data is removed within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Access tokens for messaging platforms are retained only while the channel is connected and are immediately cleared upon disconnection.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect stored data, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit.
  • Passwords stored as bcrypt hashes — never in plain text.
  • JWT-based authentication with configurable session expiry.
  • Access tokens stored server-side and never exposed to browser clients.
  • Subscription guard middleware that prevents unauthorized API access.

No system is completely secure. In the event of a data breach affecting user rights, we will notify affected users promptly.

7. Meta Platform Data

When a restaurant connects a Facebook Page, Instagram account, or WhatsApp Business number through our platform, we access only the permissions granted during the OAuth flow. Specifically:

  • We read and send messages on behalf of the connected page/account.
  • We do not access personal Facebook profiles of end-users.
  • We do not store Meta user IDs beyond what is necessary for conversation continuity.
  • Restaurants can disconnect their Meta channels at any time from the dashboard, which immediately revokes our access tokens.

Our use of Meta APIs complies with the Meta Platform Terms and the Meta Platform Policies .

8. User Rights

Restaurant owners and their customers have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete data through the dashboard.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Portability: Export your restaurant's data in a readable format.
  • Objection: Object to specific uses of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

9. Cookies

Our web dashboard uses localStorage to store authentication tokens on your device. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

10. Children's Privacy

Our platform is intended for business use by restaurant operators. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 18 years of age.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or a notice on the dashboard. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
